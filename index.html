Rolex

Seiko

Since the Tokyo Olympic Games in 1964, Seiko has proudly assisted international sport with world-leading timing technologies and services. Today, Seiko supports a wide range of sports, from track and field athletics (IAAF) to sailing (The 49er and 29erXX Associations).

Swatch

A little bit of crazy, a whole lot of love. 30 years ago, in 1990, a small group of people joined a passion for our watches and formed what was then called the “Swatch Collectors of Swatch”. Today, we're in more than 100 countries from all over the world.

Tissot

Tissot introduced its first tactile watch, with "T-Touch," technology in 1999; watches containing this technology have touch-sensitive sapphire crystals to control various functions including compass, barometer, altimeter and thermometer.
